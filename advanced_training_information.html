About the Training

Description

Our advanced training program in Craniosacral Therapy and Somatoemotional Release extends over a six month period. It consists of four Friday-Sunday intensive sessions. In this training you will learn:

  1. Additional craniosacral therapy bodywork techniques including fascial glide, energy cyst localization and release, and regional unwinding
  2. Dialoguing techniques to help you access the wisdom of the unconscious mind
  3. Full body Somatoemotional Release (SER) — the advanced form of CST, SER integrates an elegant form of bodywork with the healing power of the mind, emotions, and spirit
  4. Small group SER — an amazing experience that will transport you through time and space into new realms of healing, feeling, and consciousness

This life altering training will help you mature as a healer in ways you'd never anticipate.
